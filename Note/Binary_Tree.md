## Binary_Tree
### 定義：
- A tree whose elements have at most 2 children. 
- The only 2 children, we typically name them the left and right child.

### Tree ＆ Binary_Tree比較：
|      |Tree                    |Binary_Tree                     |
|------|:-----------------------------:|:-------------------------:|                   
|差異   |對於node的child數目沒有限制，</br>因此每個node可以有多個child|限制只有兩個child，left child和right child|
